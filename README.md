> **EPA data shows communities of color face 35% higher pollution exposure** and are more likely to live near Superfund sites and industrial facilities. Environmental review processes are inaccessible to affected residents.

## The Solution

Real-time pollution monitoring, automated public comments, permit tracking, compliance violation reporting.

## Agents

**PollutionMapper** — Census-tract environmental burden maps combining EPA EJScreen + satellite data
**AlertSystem** — Real-time pollution spike notifications to residents
**CommentGenerator** — Auto-generate plain-language public comments for environmental impact statements
**PermitTracker** — Track new industrial facility applications near vulnerable communities
**ComplianceMonitor** — Monitor violations and generate complaint filings
**HealthImpact** — Estimate health impacts at household level

## Safety

Environmental data informs, but does not replace, professional health assessments. Contact your local health department for specific concerns.

## Data Sources

EPA EJScreen, EPA ECHO, TRI, AirNow, Sentinel-5P satellite, CDC ATSDR, CalEnviroScreen
